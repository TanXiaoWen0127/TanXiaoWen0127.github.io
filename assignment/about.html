About Sarawak

Sarawak is a wonderful place. It is because it has More Nature. Sarawak has a whopping 56 totally protected areas, 37 gazetted national parks, five wildlife sanctuaries and 14 nature reserves. Its rainforests are the size of Austria. Sarawak's rainforests house one of the world's richest and most diverse ecosystems.
