Bamboo Chicken

Bamboo Chicken as known as Ayam Pansuh is a dish usually prepared by the Ibans and the Bidayuhs during festivals, especially Gawai Dayak, a celebration of thanksgiving marking a bountiful harvest. It is a traditional Sarawakian dish prepared by cooking chicken meat in a bamboo stalk, filled with water seasonings and covered with the shoots of the cassava plant.
