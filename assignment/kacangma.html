Kacangma

Kacangma Chicken or we call Motherwort Chicken, a famous Chinese Hakka dish unique to Sarawak. Traditionally eaten by local women during confinement after giving birth. It is essentially a stew of chicken with Kacangma herb and rice wine or tuak by non-Muslim cooks. It is not popular in West Malaysia and Singapore at all. It is rather unsure the reasons of its lack of popularity in other regions in this part of the world.
