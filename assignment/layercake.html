Sarawak Layer Cake

Sarawak layer cake, known as kek lapis Sarawak or kek lapis moden Sarawak in Malay, is a layered cake from the state of Sarawak in Malaysia. This cake can be found almost everywhere in the Malaysian state of Sarawak. Usually Kek Lapis Sarawak will be served on special occasions. It is layered and multi-color cake that invented by by Betawis people from Jakarta to Sarawak.
