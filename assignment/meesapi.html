Sarawak Mee Sapi

Mee sapi is a noodle dish which typically uses noodles similar to kolok mee. Most Kolok Mee in Kuching are served with minced pork and char siew as the main topping, thus rendering them non-halal. However, there are some halal eateries that managed to come up with their own version called Mee Sapi which is served with beef-based broth and topped with beansprouts and beef slices used as the substitute for pork.
