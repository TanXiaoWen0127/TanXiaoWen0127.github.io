Nuba Laya

Nuba' Laya is a type of food mashed rice wrapped in a type of leaf known as long leaf for the Iban community while for Kelabit it is called "isip" or "itip". Maybe another name for this Nuba' Laya is mashed potatoes or it can be said that it is almost similar to what is often served in fast food restaurants which is "ketang lenyek" or "mashed potato" in English. The only difference is that it is based on rice instead of potatoes.
